# $R_1$ Relaxation Rates

The $R_1$ module provides utilities to convert longitudinal relaxation times ($T_1$) into relaxation rates ($R_1$).

The fundamental relationship is $R_1 = \frac{1}{T_1}$. In MRI studies utilizing contrast agents, $R_1$ scales linearly with the tracer concentration, making it a highly convenient format for transport simulations and Concentration Maps.
